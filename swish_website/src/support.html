Support

Swish-e is a community effort. Swish-e is developed and supported completely through the efforts of volunteers. After reading the [% link_to_page( 'swish-faq', 'Swish-e FAQ' ) %], the [% link_to_page( 'docs', 'Swish-e documentation' ) %], and using the Search tool at the top of the page (which searches all documentation and the discussion archives), ponder the immortal words of Blanche Dubois in Tennessee William's A Streetcar Named Desire: "I have often relied upon the kindness of strangers."

Join the [% link_to_page('discuss', 'Swish-e discussion e-mail list') %] to ask your question. We recommend you read these good guidelines before sending mail to the list. Read [% link_to_page('install#When%20posting,%20please%20provide%20the%20following%20information:', 'these important tips') %] if you want effective and speedy help.

You might also want to check out the Swish-e Wiki site. You'll find examples of folks using Swish-e and other Swish-e related projects.
